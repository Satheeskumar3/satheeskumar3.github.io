About Nazriya_Nazim

Nazriya Nazim born 20 December 1993 is an Indian film actress, producer and former television presenter who works predominantly in Malayalam and Tamil films

She started her career as an anchor on Malayalam television channel Asianet before pursuing a career as an actress.
She made her debut as a child artist with Palunku (2006) and then as a lead actress in the 2013 Malayalam film Maad Dad. She has gone on to star in successful films such as Neram (2013), Raja Rani (2013), Ohm Shanthi Oshaana (2014), and Bangalore Days (2014),
